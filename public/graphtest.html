Chart Information

Blue Line: Continuous automated water elevation readings from monitoring equipment. Shows the natural fluctuation of water levels over time.
Red Dots: Manual verification readings taken by field personnel. These validate the automated readings and appear only when both measurements were taken on the same day.

Note: Manual readings are only displayed where they match the date of automated readings. The chart demonstrates excellent correlation between automated and manual measurements, confirming the reliability of the monitoring system.
